Can we contact you?

You can give anonymous feedback. But it helps us more if we can ask questions or check details with you. You can choose whether we phone or email you.

We'll only use your details to ask you about your feedback. We'll keep them confidential. The only exception is if you tell us about:

  • abuse or serious neglect
  • a serious crime
