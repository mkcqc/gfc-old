Cookies we use

Google Analytics cookies

Google Analytics helps us see how people use our site. It tells us things like:

  • the pages you've visited
  • how long you spent on each page
  • whether you got to the end of the form
  • how you found it - for example, if you came from a search engine or followed a link from somewhere else

Google Analytics does not show us personal information, like your name or IP address.
