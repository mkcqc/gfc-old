What happens next

What people tell us about services helps us build a picture of the standard of care they give.

We'll look at the information you've given us. How we use it will depend on what you've said. For example, if you've told us about a serious concern we'll take action, like contacting or visiting the service.

If you've given us your email address, we'll email you again to tell you when we've passed your feedback to the right person. This will be in the next 2 working days.
